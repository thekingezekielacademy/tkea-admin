# OG Image Requirements for King Ezekiel Academy

## Image Specifications:
- **Dimensions**: 1200x630 pixels (1.91:1 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Under 1MB
- **Text**: Must be readable on social media

## Required OG Images:

### 1. `/img/og-default.jpg` - Default OG Image
**Content to include:**
- King Ezekiel Academy logo
- Tagline: "Leading Digital Marketing Education Platform"
- Visual elements: Graduation cap, digital marketing icons
- Colors: Brand colors (blue, white, accent colors)

### 2. `/img/og-home.jpg` - Homepage OG Image
**Content to include:**
- Hero image with King Ezekiel
- "Transform Your Career with Digital Marketing"
- "10,000+ Successful Students"
- Call-to-action: "Start Your FREE Trial Today"

### 3. `/img/og-about.jpg` - About Page OG Image
**Content to include:**
- King Ezekiel and Blessing Adima photos
- "Meet the Team"
- "Our Mission: Democratizing Digital Marketing Education"
- Achievement highlights

### 4. `/img/og-courses.jpg` - Courses Page OG Image
**Content to include:**
- Course preview images
- "Comprehensive Digital Marketing Courses"
- "From Beginner to Expert"
- Course categories icons

### 5. `/img/og-blog.jpg` - Blog Page OG Image
**Content to include:**
- Blog post previews
- "Latest Digital Marketing Insights"
- "Expert Tips & Strategies"
- Reading glasses or document icons

## Design Guidelines:
- Use high-quality, professional imagery
- Ensure text is large enough to read on mobile
- Include brand colors consistently
- Add subtle gradients or overlays for text readability
- Test how images look on Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn

## File Naming Convention:
- Use lowercase with hyphens
- Include descriptive names
- Keep under 50 characters
- Example: `og-courses-digital-marketing.jpg`
